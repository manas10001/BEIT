LTMMC

LTMGH, (Lokmanya Tilak Municipal General Hospital), locally known as "Sion Hospital", is a general municipal hospital situated in Sion, a suburb of Mumbai. It was started in 1947 with 10 beds initially, which has now grown into multi-speciality hospital with more than 1,400 beds. In the same campus, it is attached to Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College (LTMMC) which is a teaching institute for undergraduate and post graduate studies in medical sciences.

VJTI

Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI) is an engineering college located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, and one of the oldest engineering colleges in Asia. Founded in 1887 and formerly known as the Victoria Jubilee Technical Institute, it adopted its present name on January 26, 1997. VJTI is an academically and administratively autonomous institute, however it is affiliated to the University of Mumbai.The institute is financially supported by the Government of Maharashtra.

  • FEES: 83,000/-
  • Courses Offered: B.Tech M.Tech Ph.D
  • Intake: 120
